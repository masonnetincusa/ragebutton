IMPORTANT NOTES

Users with ∞ points or Gold status can not be demoted unless they're voted out by a large majority. Medals are also called Badges. Medals are only awarded by Mason Murphy manually. There is no automatic process for awarding medals. To be awarded a medal, you have to meet it's requirements.

Gold Status

Awarded to the first users of Rage Button. This medal is no longer awarded.

Points Verified

Awarded to users that have had their points manually verified by Mason Murphy.

1st Place

Awarded to users that place first on the points leaderboard.

Admin

Awarded to employees at MASONNET.

Active

Awarded to active users on Rage Button.

Ultra Active

Awarded to extremely active users on Rage Button and masonnet.org.

Insane Clicker

Awarded to users that score more than 1B points, are ultra active, have a multi-day streak, and who click for long periods of time.

Easter Egg

Awarded to users who find a special easter egg.

Cool

Awarded to cool people! (If you ask for this, you're not getting it)

All Badges/Medals

Awarded to users who have every single medal! This includes medals that are no longer awarded, but does not include the Admin medal.
